Home Made Authentic Big Mac

Here's how to make a truly excellent burger, virtually identical to a McDonald's Big Mac only bigger and better.

Burger:

  • 3 Piece bun (heel, club, cap)
  • 2 All beef patties
  • Finely chopped white onion
  • 2 Slices of your favorite cheese
  • Shredded lettuce

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons French dressing
  • 4 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
  • 2 teaspoons American mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced white onion
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Combine sauce ingredients in a bowl and stir until completely mixed, then refrigerate.

Pan-fry beef patties, seasoning with salt and pepper.

Assemble the burger in this order (from bottom to top): Heel, sauce, patty, cheese, lettuce, club, patty, cheese, lettuce, onion, sauce, cap.
