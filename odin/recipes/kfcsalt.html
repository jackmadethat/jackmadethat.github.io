KFC-Style Chip Salt

Here's how to make salt for your hot chips that's very similar to the salt used by KFC.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons onion salt
  • 1 tablespoon chicken stock powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt

Procedure:

Combine everything together, mix well, then put in a shaker. That's it! You can also put a bit of dry white rice in the shaker to absorb moisture or it may clump or harden.
